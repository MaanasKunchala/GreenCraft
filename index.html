Custom-made Greenhouses

Our team will build greenhouses in your community, or a basic structure in an indoor space, and provide all the necessary supplies required to continue building the garden, facilitating community participation.

Community activity

This is a community-driven project! The community comes together to finish building the garden, and regularly maintain it. This promotes community engagement and socialization.

Tools for growth

All the necessary supplies and tools will be provided by us - including soil, seeds, equipment, and even weekly schedules for the community to come together and maintain the garden.
